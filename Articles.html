  Reading teaches babies how to communicate, how to listen, how to strengthen their memory, and how to learn words. The more you read to them, the more sounds they will pick up, which will aid in their learning to speak. Reading to babies also aids their emotional development.Even though a baby is unable to communicate, he or she is learning about the world around them.
